### Medical Implications of the Matrix Matrix
* **False Positives (1):** Clinically, this leads to unnecessary anxiety and secondary screening costs, but presents low clinical risk.
* **False Negatives (2):** Clinically, this represents undiagnosed sick patients being discharged without care. **This is the most critical metric to minimize in healthcare systems.**
